# Tableau Business Analysis Project

## Overview

This project was completed for a Business Analytics, Applied Modelling & Prediction module. It uses Tableau dashboards to analyse four years of sales data and identify business insights for senior management.

The report focuses on sales performance, customer metrics, shipping trends, profit margins, and profit forecasting. The main goal was to interpret dashboard results and translate them into practical business recommendations.

## Dashboard Areas

The project includes five dashboard analysis sections:

1. Comprehensive Sales Insights
2. Customer Metrics
3. Shipping Insights
4. Global Profit Insights
5. Future of Profits: Forecasts and Trends

## Tools Used

- Tableau
- Excel
- Dashboard analysis
- Data visualization

## Methods Used

- Sales analysis
- Customer analysis
- Shipping cost and shipping time analysis
- Profit margin analysis
- Forecasting and trend analysis
- Business recommendation development

## Main Findings

- The consumer segment contributed strongly to sales, with technology products showing high sales performance.
- Sales showed growth across the four-year period, with stronger performance around the fourth quarter.
- APAC showed strong sales and profit performance compared with several other markets.
- Shipping costs decreased over time, while average shipping time slightly increased.
- Technology appeared to be a key driver of profit growth.
- Profit forecasting suggested continued growth, especially in technology and high-performing markets.

## Recommendations

The report recommends focusing on technology-related products, especially in high-performing markets such as APAC. It also suggests using targeted campaigns during peak sales periods, reviewing shipping cost and delivery trade-offs, and improving market-specific sales strategies.
